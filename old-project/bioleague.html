BioLeague

BioLeague

The BioLeague competition aims to equip aspiring biologists with advanced knowledge, offering a unique experience in the field. Its mission is to inspire young investigators to delve deeper into biology through a competitive and collaborative environment during a eight-week period of intensive communication and engagement.

Apply Now

Our Journey

The BioLeague is a prestigious biology competition founded in 2021 and is currently underway. It originated at the STEM October Biology Club and had since gained remarkable momentum, expanding its influence both nationwide and globally. BioLeague offers an exciting opportunity for high school students to demonstrate their knowledge and skills in the field of biology. The Bio League Competition was meticulously crafted with the explicit goal of engaging high school students who possess an unwavering desire to excel in Biology and its related fields, offering them a unique opportunity to embark on their first strides in this scientific domain.

Eligibility

To be eligible to participate, students must be currently enrolled in grades 9-12. Additionally, teams must consist of a maximum of four students, allowing participants to work collaboratively and showcase their teamwork abilities. One of the best aspects of our competition is that there are no fees associated with joining. This means that all students, regardless of their financial situation, have the opportunity to participate and showcase their biology knowledge. We believe everyone should have the chance to pursue their passions and interests, which is why we have made our competition accessible to all. Finally, the competition is open to students of all nationalities. This provides a unique opportunity for students from different parts of the world to come together, share their perspectives, and compete in a friendly and supportive environment. We believe that diversity and inclusivity are essential components of any worthwhile endeavor, and we are committed to promoting these values in our biology competition.

About Us.

We aim to immerse young students in a dynamic environment with biology enthusiasts, motivated mentors, and abundant resources to spark their creativity and curiosity. This program offers opportunities to explore biology beyond the classroom, from the DNA of bacteria to whale anatomy. By raising awareness of biology's importance, students can hone their knowledge, conduct impactful research, and potentially contribute new information to the field.
